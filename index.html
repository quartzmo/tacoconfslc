TacoConf Salt Lake City

Coming again Spring 2014

IN DAYS HOURS MINUTES SECONDS

Details

What: Per the original TacoConf, the objective is to bicycle around Salt Lake City, learn about tacos and then eat them! Check out the 2013 Photo Gallery.

When: Saturday, May 3, 2014, 11:30 a.m.

Where: Take TRAX, FrontRunner, car or bike to meet at the GREENbike station at the SLC Intermodal Hub, 300 S 600 W.

How: A bicycle is required. Bring your own if you can as GREENbike availability will be limited.

Attire: Sombreros welcome.

Cost: Free to participate. Tacos are generally really cheap, but the last stop also offers dessert and a full bar. Apart from any GREENbike charges, depending on your appetite, you'll probably spend $5-30. Carts are cash only.

Info: Use the #tacoconfslc hashtag on Twitter.

Route: View the 2013 route on the official map.

*Disclaimer: Biking around eating street tacos is a potentially dangerous activity. Participation is strictly at your own risk.
