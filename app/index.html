TacoConf Salt Lake City

The Summer 2013 Beta* Ride

Countdown: DAYS HOURS MINUTES SECONDS

Details

What: Per the original TacoConf, the objective is to bicycle around Salt Lake City, learn about tacos and then eat them!

When: Saturday, June 22, 2013, 11:30 a.m.

Where: Take TRAX, FrontRunner, car or bike to meet at the GREENbike station at the SLC Intermodal Hub, 300 S 600 W.

How: A bicycle is required. Bring your own if you can as GREENbike availability will be limited.

Cost: Free to participate. Tacos are cheap. Apart from any GREENbike charges, depending on your appetite, you'll probably just need $5-20 cash.

Info: Use the #tacoconfslc hashtag on Twitter

*Disclaimer: This is the first time we've ever done this, and we'll be figuring it out as we go. Participation is of course at your own risk.
